In 1933, A. N. Kolmogorov published a monograph entitled Foundations of the Theory of Probability (see [1]) to lay out an axiomatic approach to probability theory. In this new framework, we generalise Kolmogorov's approach so that we may work over arbitrary fields rather than restricting ourselves to the rational or decimal number field.
